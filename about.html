About us

M-SYSTEMS is a consulting company providing services in the areas of:

  • Management Consulting
  • Information Technology Consulting
  • Procurement Services
  • Turnkey Project Management

We render outstanding services through our well established, diverse, strong, reliable, and dependable network of high achievers, highly qualified Associates, and reputable companies we are affiliated with, for the purposes of expertise pool utilisation. With annual turnover in millions of dollars, M-SYSTEMS has been in operation since 1988, and prides itself as a reliable link for purchasers of products (heavy machinery) and services from the West African region, and to North American companies that are interested in the huge opportunity the West African region, especially, Nigerian markets offer. Motivated by professional pride, each consulting-member of M-SYSTEMS is highly skilled, dedicated and capable of rendering outstanding services. M-SYSTEMS service delivery is guided by adherence to professional ethics, competence and integrity.
